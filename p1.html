An approach to mix game, social, and climate change action

Functions

  • Earn points by doing over one hundred low-carbon tasks
    • Normal task +100pts
    • Friend quest +150pts
    • Quest completed by friend +20pts
  • Use points to customize your planet
    • Drag and drop items
    • Purchase plants and animals
    • Customize background
  • Visit your friends' planet and enjoy social!
  • Publish completed task as a quest for your friends
