Peel To Heal

Internet brings us convenience, but also hurts. People tend to neglect others' feeling when they abuse them online, which is one type of cyberbullying. Our project focuses on cyberbullying happening among strangers with "equal power". By pushing the blocks back we want people to reflect on consequences of their actions toward strangers. By peeling the stickers off we hope to arise a sense to change this situation, to be an active bystander.
