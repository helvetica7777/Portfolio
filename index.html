I'm a student of the University of Miami. As an Interactive Media Graduate student, I have taken classes about coding, designing, and prototyping. What I learned from these classes are not only technical skills of designing, but also the ethics of it. My top interest lies in making fun games and interactive apps that make the world better. Impact games are games that try to transform players' cognition of the topic, and they compose the main part of my portfolio, which were produced in our courses during this year. Besides, I'm also a quick learner, and a team person who knows how to coordinate different opinions.
