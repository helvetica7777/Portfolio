Objective

In the VR game, the player is trapped in a maze with many rooms. Each room has a puppet in it, which might be quiet or aggressive. The goal is to find clues from room settings and then figure out the way to run out of the maze.

Gameplay Mechanics

The player can move within several steps in the center of each room. The mystery in each room could be solved by looking around and solve mysteries. After the player obtains the rune paper, he can use it to open the right door in order. By looking closer at the door, the player could see the shadow of puppet on the other side.

Scenes

Right now we have two scenes: The first room is brighter, larger, with nine doors to choose from. The second room is darker, smaller, with four doors around.

Controls

Use Occulus controllers to turn around and move forward.
